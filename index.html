Rolling dice is a fun way to add randomness to games, puzzles, and even creative projects. Here's a quick guide to get you started:

Standard dice: A six-sided die, also known as a d6, has numbers 1-6 on its sides.

Multi-sided dice: There are many types of multi-sided dice, such as d8, d10, d12, and d20, each with different numbers of sides.

Dice rolls: To roll a die, simply randomly select a number between 1 and the number of sides on the die.

Rolling tips: You can roll one or multiple dice at a time, and some games use special dice with different numbers or symbols on each side. Have fun
